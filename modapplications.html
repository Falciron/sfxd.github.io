Last Updated: August 8, 2018
MOD APPLICATIONS

Alright, we made it official, mod apps are officially open. Please log your applications in #mod-applications. The only permissions given in this channel are "Send Messages" and "Read Messages", but not "View Message History". Meaning you'll send the message, then see nothing. This is normal. We will go over applications and decide everything by September 15th at latest.

REQUIREMENTS

You must have joined the server long enough for us to not remember the exact date (Discord doesn’t keep account joining records)
You must be an active member of the community
You must have 2FA enabled on your discord account (mod functions are tied to it)

FREE-FORM STUFF

  1. Who are you? Interpret this as you will.
  2. How much free time do you have? Moderating requires quite a bit of time - are you able to keep up? Please detail any time constraints you have (work, school, etc)
  3. What moderating or other leadership experience do you have? What have you learned from that experience (online or off)?
  4. What has your experience with the server been? How have you contributed?
  5. Why do you want to moderate here? Why are you fit for the task?
  6. What is your moderation “style” ?
  7. What changes would you make to the discord, if any?
  8. What is your opinion on the Salesforce Discord community?
